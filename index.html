MSc in Computer Science Student @ KTH Royal Institute of Technology

My main interests lie in the intersection between Artificial Intelligence and Art, Human-Computer Interaction and Neuroscience.

Currently pursuing a MSc in Computer Science at KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Sweden).

Prior to KTH, I received my BSc in Computer Science at University of Cyprus (Cyprus) in 2021. During and after my Bachelor I worked as a Special Scientist at UCY conducting research related to Computational Neuroscience.

Previously a research intern at Immersive Technologies for Intelligent and Creative Applications (ITICA) Research Group @ CYENS Centre of Excellence (Cyprus) and HUman Bio-Behavioral Signals (HUBBS) Lab @ Texas A&M University (USA).

Aside of Computer Science, I am an active member of the International Plastic Modelers Society (IPMS) Cyprus, interested in cycling, film photography and philosophy of mind.
